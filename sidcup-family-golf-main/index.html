ABOUT US

We are a team of technology enthusiasts who believe in making social media a safer place for everyone. Our project, "Identify User Behind Social Media Based on Drug Trafficking," is focused on using machine learning to help identify people involved in drug trafficking on social media. Our goal is to create tools that can spot patterns in posts, behavior, and connections that may point to illegal activities.
